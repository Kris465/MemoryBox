Ch. 1

After Transmigrating, She Became A Little Widow In A Farming Family

"Kukuduludu-kukuruku..."

A loud crowing of a rooster broke the silence of the village at the foot of the mountain.

Soon, the roosters in the village began to act as alarm clocks, urging the farmers to get up quickly.

Fortunately, not every household has a rooster; otherwise, the excitement would be comparable to a large-scale concert, and the listeners would be excited.

After the crowing of the rooster, the lights in the villagers' homes were lit one after another, and occasionally there were a few light voices. Soon, smoke began to come out of the chimneys of a few quick-handed families, which made the originally silent village suddenly have a hint of fireworks.

Li Suniang also opened her eyes to the sound of roosters crowing. Now she is used to this kind of life of being woken up by the rooster alarm clock.

When she first came here, she was really disturbed by the chickens. She couldn't sleep any longer, and she wanted to kill all the chickens.

You see, she is a person who wakes up easily. It's hard for her to fall asleep at night. Just when she was about to fall asleep, she was woken up by the chickens. Don't you think she will be upset?

But it is unknown if it's because she changed places and the magnetic field is different, or maybe there is not so much pressure and burden in her heart. Gradually, her sleep quality has improved, and she is not easily awoken in the middle of the night. 

It's only been a month now. Now she can almost say that she can sleep as long as she sticks to the bed, and she can sleep until the cock crows, and she has no dreams all night. It's better than the sleeping pills she took in her previous life.

To say that what the ancients said is absolutely correct, resting at sunset and working at sunrise is actually the best health therapy.

It's a pity that in her previous life, even people in the countryside would no longer have such a life. Except for those in their seventies and eighties, who, because of their age, have less sleep time at night, which leads to getting up early in the morning.

However, those old people go to bed early. Almost no old people go to bed after ten o'clock.

With the continuous advancement of time, the pace of urban construction is getting faster and faster, and the pace of technology is getting faster and faster. People's living standards are getting better and better, but the pressure that comes with it is also getting greater and greater.

People's desires are getting bigger and bigger, endless desires, and the deep pit of desires that cannot be filled. People's hearts are becoming more and more restless and greedy. There are also many problems in physical health, and various kinds of problems are increasing. Among them, sub-health is already the best. There are more and more young people suffering from mental illness, especially depression.

So everything has two sides, good and bad.

One shouldn't be obsessed and take hasty steps; slow down and, take a breath, let your body and mind relax a little more. The future will not run, but time will be a little longer, maybe there will be a more perfect harvest.

Li Suniang, who had a good night's sleep, stretched gently and, as usual, looked to the left at the child who was still sleeping soundly, and the corners of her mouth were slightly raised. A beautiful day began again.

She quietly lifted a corner of the quilt, got out of bed quietly, put on her clothes, turned around, and tucked the quilt in gently, making sure the child in the quilt would not be cold. Then quietly opened the door and walked out, and then quietly closed the door.

She turned around and took a deep breath.

"Huh..."

The air here is still fresh! In the previous life, the rural areas, compared with the prosperous city, the air quality is indeed much better, but compared with the ancient times, it is still unknown how much worse.

Today's Li Suniang is actually not the original one. The original one has gone to report to the King of Hell. As for why? Today's Li Suniang doesn't know, she only knows that she was already in this body when she woke up and opened her eyes.

But from the words of Aunt Yang next door when she tried to comfort her, it seemed that the original one probably couldn't accept it. The man who had always loved her passed away suddenly, without even leaving a word. 

The death of the man-made her unable to bear it in her heart, and she was always depressed, and then she became ill. She died of illness without anyone knowing, and then the current Li Suniang came.

One soul left, and another soul came. There was almost no deviation between the replacements, so no one would doubt that the current Li Suniang had a new soul.

At most, people think that the current Li Suniang is much stronger than before. The previous one was too weak as if she could fall with a gust of wind. She would blush if you looked at her a few more times. If you spoke to her loudly, you would feel that tears would come out.

She was beautiful but wasn't suitable to be a farmer’s wife. The villagers didn’t know where Niu Da found such a wife. Seeing how Niu Da loved his wife like a treasure, she was the envy of wives of all sizes in the village, and of course, there were also sour words. But the original Li Suniang didn’t go out, and she could hardly hear those words. 

Speaking of Niu Da, although his name is a bit too sloppy, the first impression people get is that he is a rough man who doesn’t look very good.

Actually, this is not the case. According to Aunt Yang next door, Niu Da is actually a good-looking man. Although he is not very handsome, he is considered good-looking in the village. He is about 1.7 meters tall and capable. He has several acres of good land at home and a good job in the town. Moreover, there is no pressure from the elderly at home. She was immediately the master of the family after marriage.

According to Aunt Yang, there were many matchmakers for Niu Da at the beginning. Several girls in this village wanted to marry him. Some girls even deliberately threw themselves into Niu Da's arms, but unfortunately, Niu Da didn't like any of them.

Later, Niu Da brought back Li Suniang. When he brought Li Suniang back, several girls in the village who secretly liked Niu Da were jealous to death. They cursed Li Suniang as a vixen behind her back. There was even a girl who was unwilling to give up and said some sarcastic words to Li Suniang, which scared Li Suniang sick.

Don't look at Niu Da, he usually has a good temper, but that's not his weakness. When he learned that his wife, whom he treated as a treasure, was bullied, he was furious. After arranging for his wife to fall asleep, he immediately rushed to the girl's house and made a fierce operation, which scared the family to turn around and leave when they saw Niu Da in the future, especially the girl, who was almost unable to survive after being scolded by him.

So one can not judge a person by his appearance, as one can not measure the sea by a bucket. Don't look at Niu Da's gentle appearance, that's just because he thinks that people are dispensable in his eyes, and the people who are really in his heart must never be bullied.

Since then, Niu Da has also quit his job in the town, concentrated on farming at home, and took care of his beautiful and weak wife at home, keeping the small family clean and tidy, and the young couple lived a harmonious life. The next year, a son was born, which made Niu Da very happy, and he kept shouting that their Niu family had an heir.

Since Li Suniang gave birth to a child, Niu Da has taken even more care of them and has made Li Suniang even more beautiful than when she first came.

As the child grew up, Li Suniang also began to learn to do housework, from being flustered at the beginning to becoming more and more capable later.

When the child called him father for the first time, Li Suniang also successfully took over the housework. Although the food she cooked was not as delicious as Niu Da's, as long as it was cooked by her, Niu Da would always eat it up with satisfaction, and every time after eating, he would be in a good mood for the whole day, as if he had picked up a gold ingot.

Of course, Li Suniang's work was limited to housework. As for the work in the fields outside, Niu Da was reluctant to let her do it. Even when farming was the busiest, he would, at most, let her bring water to the fields for him to drink and then send her back again for fear that she would be sunburned. Sometimes Niu Da brought the water himself and didn't ask her to bring it at all.

The day when the child could call dad, Niu Da was so happy that he almost flew to the sky. He took his son out and told everyone he met that his son could call dad. That night, Niu Da was so happy that he almost tossed Li Suniang to death, so Li Suniang didn't get up until noon the next day.

As time went by, the child could walk and run slowly. The original warm family of two became a family of three with childish talk and more laughter.

The man was responsible for the outside and the woman was responsible for the inside. A perfect family was moving more and more towards a happy and perfect road. But who knew that an accident that no one knew about happened? How could a person who was obviously very healthy just disappear without any signs?

It was just like a dream that he went to the field as usual, he fell down and never opened his eyes again until he died.

Niu Da's death was like pulling out the backbone of Li Suniang, making her feel that there was no hope for her future life, and the future became dark, which made her lose hope of living all of a sudden. If it weren't for Aunt Yang and her daughter-in-law who kept persuading her and often pulled the child to her side, she would probably have gone with Niu Da on the day he passed away.

But even so, it still couldn't bring back her dead heart. She still fell ill with depression and left this world silently, leaving the child who once gave her maternal love to pursue the man who loved her.

Fortunately, her departure did not bring discomfort and trouble to anyone, nor did it bring doubts to anyone. In short, her departure was equivalent to not leaving. After all, Li Suniang was still alive, just the same person with a different soul.

If the child was a few years older, perhaps he would find something different. But the child is still young, and he was just confused as to why his mother was sleeping all the time and would not wake up no matter how he called her, even when he pushed her. He was a little scared that his mother would be like his father, but fortunately, his mother woke up later and did not abandon him as his father did.

The author has something to say: This article does not have any golden fingers, nor does it have any space. It is just a story about a person who transmigrates and marries and has children like ordinary people. The couple respects and loves each other and builds a warm and harmonious home together.
